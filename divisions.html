Divisions

Engineering Department

Mechanical Engineering: The mechanical engineering department works closely with the CAD/Design department to determine the materials needed to build the overall structure of the robot, including any additional parts necessary for competition or outreach events. During build season, the mechanical department works extensively on the robot to ensure maximum efficiency.

Programming: The programming department is responsible for creating the robot’s software, including primary and secondary controls. They are also responsible for the software of different technology demos in outreach event

Electrical Engineering: The electrical department is responsible for the wiring of the computer systems of the robot and the testing of its electrical systems. Additionally, they are responsible for keeping all electrical wiring organized.

Business Department

Public Relations: The public relations department is responsible for the management of the team’s social media pages and the team website. Their duties include posting updates, pictures, and videos. They also are responsible for the production of the fundraising media and event.

Grants and Sponsorships: This department is responsible for filling out applications for grants and sponsorships. They also find fundraising oppurtunitites for the team and manage the team finances.

Internal Operations: The operations department handles the logistics of our outreach events, such as the venue, amount of supplies, etc, including finding volunteers. The department also communicates extensively with the finance department to ensure sufficient funds for each event.
